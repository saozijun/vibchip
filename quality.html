Initial Visual Inspection

Our Quality Control (QC) team conducts comprehensive visual inspections of all electronic components, both internally and externally.

  • Documentation Verification: Meticulously reviewing shipping documents, packing lists, invoices and delivery orders to ensure administrative accuracy.
  • Packaging Inspection: Examining outer cartons, inner boxes, vacuum packaging, pallets, tape reels, and ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) protection measures.
  • Label Verification: Verifying the accuracy of label information, including product names, part numbers, quantities, date codes, and traceability codes.

Engineering Validation

To ensure uncompromising reliability, we subject every component to strict Lead Inspection, Scratch Testing, Acetone Resistance Testing, and Marking Permanency Verification.

  • Label Re-verification: We cross-reference incoming labels against our system database and golden samples to ensure 100% data accuracy.
  • Marking Re-inspection: Markings are validated through rigorous solvent (chemical) resistance and scratch testing to ensure permanence.
  • Component Re-validation: We meticulously inspect and compare the casing, leads, and dimensions against strict specifications.
  • X-Ray Inspection: We utilize advanced X-ray technology to examine the die, package structure, and bonding wires, comparing them against our golden samples for structural integrity.

Electrical Performance & Environmental Compliance Testing

We combine cutting-edge industry technology with insights derived from our proprietary internal database.

  • Solderability Testing
  • Decapsulation Analysis
  • Electrical Performance Testing
  • RoHS Compliance Verification

Validation Through Accredited Partnerships

  • Our commitment to providing safe, reliable, and flawless products is underpinned by our proprietary QC laboratory.
  • Additionally, we collaborate with leading international testing partners such as CECC Laboratories, White Horse, and Global ETS to ensure compliance with the highest industry standards.

Supplier Qualification

Vibchip's supplier qualification process rigorously vets potential electronic component manufacturers and suppliers, reinforcing anti-counterfeit prevention and avoidance measures at every step.

Supplier Selection

Prior to adding any supplier to our Approved Vendor List (AVL), we conduct a thorough vetting of their reputation, performance, and operational standards. Our process includes a rigorous review of the potential partner's industry standing, track record, and references.

Enforcing Quality Standards

Our supplier partners must consistently meet our stringent quality standards to maintain their status in our Approved Vendor List (AVL). We foster open and honest communication to proactively address challenges and ensure a mutually beneficial partnership.

Authentic, New and Original

Vibchip takes every precaution to ensure customers receive authorized, genuine components sourced directly from the Original Component Manufacturers (OCMs) or their authorized distributors.

Supplier Rating System

Following an extensive qualification process, our network of over 3,000 certified suppliers is organized into a performance-based quality tier system. This structure prioritizes sourcing from suppliers with the lowest risk profiles and the highest quality standards.
